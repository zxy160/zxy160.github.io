Demo Article 1: Tree

This demo page validates a single left navigation tree for article switching and section jumping.

Overview

The left tree keeps sibling articles and the current article headings in one structure, so readers do not have to split their attention between two navigation systems.

Why One Tree

The UEFI-style reading model works because article switching and in-page movement live in the same left rail instead of competing panels.

Layout Boundary

The content column stays focused on the article body while the left rail remains the primary document navigation.

Fallback Rule

If the manifest cannot load, the page should still present readable article content without collapsing the layout into an error state.
