Demo Article 2: Hash

This demo page validates hash highlighting and previous-next navigation across article pages.

Article Switch

When a reader moves between sibling notes pages, the active article entry should switch with the page and keep the left tree consistent.

Hash Highlight

When the URL points at a section anchor, the matching child item in the left tree should become the active location.

Previous Next

The footer navigation should follow manifest order so the notes set reads like a small document sequence instead of a blog timeline.

Mobile Fallback

On narrow screens the tree stacks above the article, but section targeting and previous-next links should remain readable and usable.
